3DR Dataset


Introduction

We introduce a new dataset of 3D reconstruction, including the monocular data and large-scale city data captured by mobile device and drone respectively.



Mobile 3D Reconstruction

The mobile 3d reconstruction dataset includes the Scenes and Objects, both of them follow dior format and we also show the texture models generated by SenseMARS platform.
